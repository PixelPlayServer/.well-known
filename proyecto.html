üìã
AWARENESS POSTERS
Create informative and colorful posters with practical water-saving tips and facts. Place them strategically in bathrooms, near water fountains, and in hallways where students can see them daily and learn proper water habits.
ü§ù
WATER MONITORS
Train responsible student volunteers to become "Water Monitors" who help educate their peers about proper water use, report maintenance problems, and recognize classes with the best water-saving behavior.
üèÜ
WATER CHALLENGE
Organize exciting weekly competitions between classes to see who can save the most water. Track progress, post leaderboards, and give prizes to motivate all students to participate actively.
